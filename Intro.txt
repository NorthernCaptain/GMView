
      Welcome to Knowhere GPS and public mapping software.

Know Where - is a GPS software with ability for viewing Goo maps, 
downloading and caching them. Later you can browse your maps without 
having Internet connection, use your GPS receiver for knowing 
where you are now, recording tracks in GPS eXchange format.
